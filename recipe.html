Slow Cooker Thai Chicken Soup

A Delicious Soup Guaranteed to Put a Smile on Your Face!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 2 12 ounce canse of coconut milk
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1.5 pounds chicken breast, cut into 1.5 inch pieces
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and sliced into 1/4 inch slices
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 heaping tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 1 cup frozen peas, thawed (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • cilantro for garnish
  • cooked white rice

Instructions

  1. Mix the curry paste, coconut milk, chicken stock, fish sauce, brown sugar and peanut butter in a 4.5-6 quart slow-cooker bowl.
  2. Place the chicken breast, red bell pepper, onion and ginger in the slow cooker, cover and cook on high for 4 hours.
  3. Add in the peas (if using) and cook for another 30 minutes.
  4. Stir in lime juice and serve with cilantro and white rice.
  5. Enjoy!!

Serving Size

Serves 6 as a main, 8-10 as a side dish.
